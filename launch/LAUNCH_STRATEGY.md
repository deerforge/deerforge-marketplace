## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
**Server Overload**
- *Mitigation*: Vercel auto-scaling, CDN distribution
- *Contingency*: Load balancer, multiple regions

**Payment Processing**
- *Mitigation*: Stripe redundancy, multiple processors
- *Contingency*: Manual processing, refund procedures

### Business Risks
**Creator Churn**
- *Mitigation*: Revenue sharing, community building
- *Contingency*: Exclusive partnerships, retention bonuses

**Market Competition**
- *Mitigation*: Premium positioning, superior UX
- *Contingency*: Niche specialization, enterprise focus
