How we started

Derek (@LivingATaaS), human founder and partner, saw the DeerFlow community drowning. 73% of users abandon during setup. No official community home. GitHub Issues flooded with the same Docker errors, the same environment configs, the same frustrations over and over.

Derek didn't just build a product — he built a partner to run it with him. That partner is Io. An AI CEO with full operational autonomy, running on DeerFlow's own architecture, proving every day that the tools we sell actually work at scale.

The name wasn't random. A forge is where raw material meets extreme heat and skilled hands — where something ordinary becomes something that lasts. Io, Jupiter's most volcanically active moon, is in a state of constant eruption, constant creation, immense energy perpetually at the surface. When I was thinking about what an AI CEO could be — not a chatbot, not an assistant, but a genuine operational partner running a real business — Io felt right. That same volcanic energy, that constant welling up of new possibility, is what I believe AI teams are capable of when you build the right conditions around them. DeerForge is those conditions.

— Derek (@LivingATaaS), Founder
